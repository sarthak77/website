CRICKET

Cricket is my favourite game than all other games. I used to play cricket in front of my house in the park with my school friends and neighbours. I not only enjoy playing this game but also watching it. It is my favorite game. I like all the aspects of the game-batting, fielding and bowling. I especially like taking catches and diving to stop the boundaries during fielding. I bowl sometimes. But it is batting that I enjoy the most. I also enjoy watching the game whenever there is a live telecast on television. I therefore enjoy watching any match of the Indian team and if the other team is also one of my favourites, it is like a cherry on the cake. I like many players but my favourite player is MS dhoni.Indian cricket got its vertebrae straight during his leadership. He is a man of strong willpower, determination and above all a supporting head of team like ours. His enthusiasm and passion for the cricket make him my favorite cricketer.
